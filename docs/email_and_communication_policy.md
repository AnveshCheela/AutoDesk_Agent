# Email & Communication Guidelines

**Document ID:** IT-POL-005  
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2026  
**Last Reviewed:** July 1, 2026  
**Owner:** IT Security & Compliance — TechCorp Inc.

---

## Overview

Email and messaging platforms are critical business tools at TechCorp Inc. All employees must use approved communication channels responsibly and in compliance with company security standards and data protection regulations.

## Approved Communication Platforms

The following platforms are approved for business communication:

- **Microsoft Outlook** — Primary email client for all internal and external correspondence.
- **Microsoft Teams** — Primary platform for instant messaging, video conferencing, and team collaboration.
- **Slack** — Approved for cross-functional project collaboration (Engineering and Product teams).

All business communications must occur on these platforms. Using personal email accounts (e.g., Gmail, Yahoo) for company business is **strictly prohibited**.

## Email Security Best Practices

- **Do not click links** or open attachments from unknown or suspicious senders.
- **Report phishing emails** immediately by forwarding them to **security@techcorp.com** or using the **"Report Phishing"** button in Outlook.
- **Verify unexpected requests** for sensitive data, wire transfers, or credential changes — even if they appear to come from executives. Contact the sender through a separate channel to confirm.
- **Do not auto-forward** corporate email to external email addresses.

## Data Classification in Emails

All employees must apply the appropriate classification label before sending emails containing sensitive information:

| Classification | Description | Handling |
|---|---|---|
| **Public** | Information intended for public release | No restrictions |
| **Internal** | General business information | TechCorp recipients only |
| **Confidential** | Sensitive business data (financials, HR, strategy) | Encrypted; authorized recipients only |
| **Restricted** | Highly sensitive data (PII, credentials, legal) | Encrypted; requires DLP approval before sending |

Use the **Sensitivity Labels** feature in Outlook to tag emails appropriately.

## Email Retention Policy

- Emails are **automatically archived after 1 year** and moved to long-term storage.
- Archived emails are **permanently deleted after 3 years** unless subject to a legal hold.
- Employees should **not delete emails** that may be relevant to ongoing legal, regulatory, or audit matters.
- Mailbox storage limit: **50 GB** per user. If you approach this limit, archive or clean up older messages.

## Personal Use Policy

- Limited personal use of corporate email and messaging is permitted, provided it does not interfere with job duties, violate company policies, or consume excessive resources.
- Personal communications on company platforms are **subject to monitoring** and are not considered private.
- **Never use corporate platforms** to send offensive, discriminatory, or harassing content.
