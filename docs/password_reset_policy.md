# Password Reset Policy

**Document ID:** IT-POL-001  
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2026  
**Last Reviewed:** July 1, 2026  
**Owner:** IT Security Team — TechCorp Inc.

---

## Overview

All TechCorp Inc. employees must adhere to the following password policy to protect corporate systems and data. Passwords are the first line of defense against unauthorized access.

## How to Reset Your Password

1. **Self-Service Portal:** Visit [https://passwordreset.techcorp.com](https://passwordreset.techcorp.com) and follow the on-screen instructions. You must have previously registered your recovery phone number or email.
2. **IT Helpdesk:** If self-service is unavailable, contact the IT Helpdesk by submitting a ticket at [https://helpdesk.techcorp.com](https://helpdesk.techcorp.com) or calling extension **4350**. Identity verification will be required before a reset is processed.

## Password Requirements

All passwords must meet the following complexity requirements:

- **Minimum length:** 12 characters
- **Must include:** at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character (e.g., `!@#$%^&*`)
- **Cannot reuse** any of your last 10 passwords
- **Cannot contain** your username, display name, or common dictionary words

## Password Expiry

- Passwords **expire every 90 days**. You will receive email reminders at 14 days, 7 days, and 1 day before expiration.
- Expired passwords must be changed immediately upon next login.

## Account Lockout Policy

- After **5 consecutive failed login attempts**, your account will be **automatically locked** for 30 minutes.
- If you are locked out, wait 30 minutes or contact the IT Helpdesk for an immediate unlock.

## Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

- **MFA is mandatory** for all employees across all corporate applications.
- Supported MFA methods include the **Microsoft Authenticator** app (recommended), SMS verification, and hardware security keys (YubiKey).
- MFA enrollment is required during onboarding. If you need to re-enroll, contact IT Security at **security@techcorp.com**.

## Non-Compliance

Failure to comply with this policy may result in restricted system access and escalation to your department manager. Repeated violations will be reported to HR.
