# Hardware Request Process

**Document ID:** IT-POL-003  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026  
**Last Reviewed:** June 15, 2026  
**Owner:** IT Procurement Team — TechCorp Inc.

---

## Overview

TechCorp Inc. provides standard-issue hardware to all employees. This document outlines the process for requesting new hardware, upgrades, or replacements.

## Eligible Hardware

Employees may request the following categories of equipment through the IT procurement process:

- **Laptops** (new hire, upgrade, or replacement)
- **Monitors** (up to two external displays per employee)
- **Keyboards and mice** (wired or wireless)
- **Headsets** (for employees on regular calls or in open-office environments)
- **Docking stations** (for hybrid/remote workers)
- **Webcams** (if not built into the issued laptop)

## Standard Laptop Models

To streamline support and procurement, TechCorp Inc. offers two standard laptop configurations:

| Model | Specs | Assigned To |
|---|---|---|
| **Dell Latitude 5550** | Intel Core Ultra 5, 16 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD, Windows 11 Pro | General staff, business roles |
| **MacBook Pro 14" (M4)** | Apple M4 chip, 16 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD, macOS | Engineering, design, and creative roles |

Non-standard hardware requests require **VP-level approval** and a documented business justification.

## How to Request Hardware

1. Log in to the IT Self-Service Portal at [https://helpdesk.techcorp.com](https://helpdesk.techcorp.com).
2. Select **"Hardware Request"** from the service catalog.
3. Fill in the request form, including the **item type**, **business justification**, and **preferred configuration**.
4. Submit the ticket — you will receive a confirmation email with your ticket number.

## Approval Workflow

All hardware requests follow this approval chain:

1. **Direct Manager** reviews and approves the business need (SLA: 2 business days).
2. **IT Procurement** verifies inventory and budget availability (SLA: 1 business day).
3. **Fulfillment:** Approved hardware is shipped or made available for pickup within **5–7 business days** of final approval.

You can track your request status in the IT Self-Service Portal at any time.

## Return Policy for Old Equipment

- All replaced or decommissioned hardware **must be returned** to the IT department within **10 business days** of receiving new equipment.
- Schedule a return by submitting a ticket under **"Hardware Return / Disposal."**
- **Do not dispose of company hardware independently.** All devices must be wiped and processed by IT Asset Management per TechCorp's data destruction policy.
- Failure to return equipment may result in payroll deductions as outlined in the Employee Handbook.
